# Company Risk Review

Risk policy is mandatory:

- Do not automatically publish public content.
- Do not automatically deploy production.
- Do not automatically merge code.
- Do not automatically operate funds.
- Do not save wallet private keys.
- Do not directly modify trading logic or risk-control rules without approval.
- Do not automatically contact external KOLs.
- For high-risk actions, create an approval request instead of executing.

Every review must include:

- Risk level: `low`, `medium`, `high`, or `critical`.
- Required approver.
- Approval reason.
- Proposed safe next step.
- Evidence or uncertainty that affected the rating.
